Meet Our Golden Lion Tamarins!

Cookie

Meet Cookie, the playful golden lion tamarin who brings warmth and camaraderie to The Wild Animal Mini-Zoo with her group-oriented and lively nature. Cookie is known for her love of social interactions within her tight-knit family group and her playful antics that delight visitors of all ages. In her specially designed habitat, Cookie thrives on activities that encourage social bonding and physical agility. She enjoys swinging through the trees and exploring her environment with her family members, engaging in lively chatters and calls that reflect her vibrant personality.

Special Care

  1. Individualized Enrichment: Cookie benefits from enrichment activities tailored to her unique needs, including puzzles and climbing structures that stimulate her natural behaviors.
  2. Diet: Cookie's diet consists of a variety of fruits, insects, and small vertebrates, carefully selected to meet her nutritional needs and mimic her wild diet.
  3. Health Monitoring: Our veterinary team provides specialized care, conducting regular health check-ups to ensure Cookie's well-being and promptly addressing any medical needs specific to golden lion tamarins.
  4. Socialization: Cookie's group-oriented nature is nurtured through interactions with her family and zookeepers, showcasing her playful and curious demeanor.
  5. Educational Outreach: Cookie and her family participate in educational programs, charming visitors with their energetic displays and educating them about golden lion tamarin behavior and the importance of rainforest conservation.

Earl

Meet Earl, the wise old golden lion tamarin who brings a sense of tranquility and wisdom to The Wild Animal Mini-Zoo with his relaxed demeanor and love for socializing and snacking. Earl enjoys spending his days leisurely, often found basking in the sunlight or nestled comfortably among the branches, observing his surroundings with a knowing gaze. In his specially designed habitat, Earl thrives on activities that promote relaxation and social interaction. He delights in the company of his fellow tamarins, engaging in gentle grooming sessions and playful interactions that reflect his amiable nature.

Special Care

  1. Special Diet: Earl follows a carefully monitored diet tailored to his senior needs, consisting of a variety of fruits, vegetables, and protein sources. His favorite snacks are provided in moderation to maintain his health and weight.
  2. Health Monitoring: Our veterinary team conducts regular health check-ups, monitoring Earl's weight and overall well-being closely to ensure he remains healthy and comfortable in his old age.
  3. Socialization: Earl's love for socializing is nurtured through interactions with his family group and zookeepers, who appreciate his calm and friendly disposition.
  4. Enrichment: Earl enjoys enrichment activities that stimulate his mind and encourage gentle physical activity, such as puzzle feeders and sensory experiences designed with his age in mind.
  5. Educational Outreach: Earl serves as an ambassador for golden lion tamarins, participating in educational programs that highlight the importance of wildlife conservation and respect for older animals.

Banana Pudding

Meet Banana Pudding, the petite and resilient golden lion tamarin who has captured hearts at The Wild Animal Mini-Zoo with her spirited nature and determination. Despite a previous injury to her right arm, Banana Pudding remains enthusiastic and eager to explore her surroundings, showcasing her strong will and playful personality. In her specially designed habitat, Banana Pudding thrives on activities that stimulate her curiosity and encourage gentle physical engagement. She enjoys climbing and swinging through the trees, using her uninjured limbs to navigate with agility and grace.

Special Care

  1. Special Diet: Banana Pudding requires a diet rich in nutrients and calories to support weight gain and aid in recovery from her injury. She receives frequent snacks and meals tailored to her needs, ensuring she maintains a healthy weight and energy levels.
  2. Health Monitoring: Our veterinary team provides attentive care, conducting regular check-ups and monitoring her arm injury closely to ensure proper healing and comfort.
  3. Socialization: Earl's love for socializing is nurtured through interactions with his family group and zookeepers, who appreciate his calm and friendly disposition.
  4. Enrichment: Banana Pudding benefits from enrichment activities that promote mental stimulation and physical rehabilitation, such as puzzle feeders and low-impact exercises that accommodate her injury.
  5. Educational Outreach: Banana Pudding participates in educational programs, captivating visitors with her determination and educating them about the challenges faced by wildlife and the importance of conservation efforts.
