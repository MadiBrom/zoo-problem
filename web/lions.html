Meet Our Lions!

Mella

Meet Mella, the regal lioness who commands respect at The Wild Animal Mini-Zoo with her prideful and elegant demeanor. Mella exudes confidence with every graceful movement, her mane flowing majestically in the breeze. Despite her proud nature, Mella is also food-motivated, particularly fond of beef as a treat. In her spacious habitat, Mella engages in activities that showcase her intelligence and physical prowess. She enjoys solitary moments of contemplation but also appreciates interactions with zoo staff who understand and respect her royal presence.

Special Care

  1. Interactive Enrichment: Mella thrives on activities that stimulate her hunting instincts and problem-solving abilities, fostering a strong connection with her caregivers.
  2. Socialization: Mella interacts selectively with trusted zoo staff, displaying her elegant and confident personality, especially during feeding times with beef treats.
  3. Health Monitoring: Our veterinary team provides vigilant care, conducting regular health assessments to ensure Mella's well-being and promptly addressing any medical concerns.

Karl

Meet Karl, the charismatic male lion who brings joy and curiosity to The Wild Animal Mini-Zoo with his picky, playful nature and love for water. Karl is known for his discerning tastes and will often show a preference for certain foods, particularly enjoying beef as a treat. In his expansive habitat, Karl delights in activities that highlight his intelligence and physical prowess, especially those involving water. He is playful and enjoys splashing around in his pool or interacting with water features designed to stimulate his natural behaviors.

Special Care

  1. Interactive Enrichment: Karl thrives on enrichment activities that challenge his mind and physical abilities, particularly those involving water play. These activities strengthen his bond with his caregivers.
  2. Socialization: Karl enjoys interacting with zoo staff who understand his playful personality and appreciate his love for water. These interactions showcase his friendly and curious nature.
  3. Health Monitoring: Our veterinary team provides attentive care, conducting regular health check-ups to ensure Karl's well-being and promptly addressing any medical needs.
  4. Educational Outreach: Karl participates enthusiastically in educational programs, captivating visitors with his playful antics and educating them about lion behavior and the importance of conservation.
