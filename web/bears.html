Meet Our Grizzly Bears!

Ollie

Ollie is our distinguished grizzly bear resident at The Wild Animal Mini-Zoo. With a striking presence and a shimmering fur coat, Ollie prefers to be cared for with a respectful distance. Despite his captive environment, he thrives in his enriched habitat, enjoying interactions with specially designed toys and engaging in activities that stimulate his natural instincts.

Special Care

  1. Enriched Habitat: Ollie's enclosure is designed to mimic natural environments, with ample space to roam and explore. It includes features like climbing structures, pools for swimming, and areas for foraging.
  2. Nutritious Diet: Ollie's diet is carefully monitored and consists of a balanced mix of fruits, vegetables, nuts, fish, and occasionally meat to meet his nutritional needs.
  3. Veterinary Care: Regular check-ups and health assessments are conducted by our veterinary team to monitor Ollie's physical health and address any medical concerns promptly.
  4. Behavioral Enrichment: To keep Ollie mentally stimulated, we provide a variety of toys, puzzles, and sensory experiences that encourage natural behaviors such as foraging, climbing, and exploring.
  5. Visitor Interaction: While Ollie prefers to observe visitors from a distance, educational programs and guided tours allow guests to learn about grizzly bears and the importance of conservation efforts.

Mona

Meet Mona, the beloved grizzly bear who calls The Wild Animal Mini-Zoo home. Mona captivates with her gentle demeanor and playful nature. Unlike Ollie, Mona delights in interacting closely with zoo staff, forming bonds that enrich her life in captivity. In her enriched habitat, Mona eagerly engages with specially designed toys and activities that stimulate her curious nature. She enjoys playful interactions with her caretakers, eagerly anticipating their visits for enrichment sessions and training exercises.

Special Care

  1. Interactive Enrichment: Mona thrives on interactive toys and games that challenge her intelligence and physical abilities, fostering a strong bond with her caregivers.
  2. Socialization: Regular interactions with zoo staff allow Mona to express her friendly personality, showcasing her natural curiosity and affectionate demeanor.
  3. Health Monitoring: Our veterinary team provides comprehensive care, conducting regular health check-ups to ensure Mona's well-being and addressing any medical needs promptly.
  4. Educational Outreach: Mona participates enthusiastically in educational programs, charming visitors with her friendly disposition and helping raise awareness about grizzly bear conservation.
