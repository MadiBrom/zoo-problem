Meet Our Grizzly Bears!

Ollie

Ollie is our distinguished grizzly bear resident at The Wild Animal Mini-Zoo. With a striking presence and a shimmering fur coat, Ollie prefers to be cared for with a respectful distance. Despite his captive environment, he thrives in his enriched habitat, enjoying interactions with specially designed toys and engaging in activities that stimulate his natural instincts.

***************************************************

Mona

Meet Mona, the beloved grizzly bear who calls The Wild Animal Mini-Zoo home. Mona captivates with her gentle demeanor and playful nature. Unlike Ollie, Mona delights in interacting closely with zoo staff, forming bonds that enrich her life in captivity. In her enriched habitat, Mona eagerly engages with specially designed toys and activities that stimulate her curious nature. She enjoys playful interactions with her caretakers, eagerly anticipating their visits for enrichment sessions and training exercises.
