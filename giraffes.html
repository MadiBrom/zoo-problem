Meet Our Giraffes!

Frankie

Meet Frankie, the gentle giant giraffe who adds a touch of grace to The Wild Animal Mini-Zoo. Frankie is known for their shy demeanor and endearing food-motivated nature. Despite their initial reserve, Frankie eagerly engages in interactive enrichment activities that challenge both their intelligence and physical abilities, especially when treats are involved. In their enriched habitat, Frankie delights in specially designed toys and activities that stimulate their curious nature. They enjoy quiet interactions with zoo staff, showcasing a gentle and inquisitive personality when offered their favorite treats.

Special Care

  1. Interactive Enrichment: Frankie thrives on interactive activities that encourage problem-solving and physical engagement, strengthening their bond with caregivers.
  2. Socialization: Gradual interactions with zoo staff allow Frankie to express their unique personality, revealing a gentle and food-motivated nature.
  3. Health Monitoring: Our veterinary team provides diligent care, conducting regular health check-ups to ensure Frankie's well-being and promptly addressing any medical needs.
  4. Educational Outreach: Frankie participates in educational programs, captivating visitors with their graceful movements and raising awareness about giraffe conservation.

Coconut

Meet Coconut, the charismatic giraffe who adds a touch of humor to The Wild Animal Mini-Zoo. Coconut is known for their often grumpy demeanor, but their mood brightens considerably when offered their favorite treat: spinach. Despite their occasional grumpiness, Coconut eagerly engages in interactive enrichment activities that challenge both their intelligence and physical abilities, especially when spinach is involved. In their enriched habitat, Coconut delights in specially designed toys and activities that stimulate their curious nature. They have a particular fondness for quiet interactions with zoo staff, revealing a more playful and content side when rewarded with spinach treats.

Special Care

  1. Interactive Enrichment: Coconut thrives on activities that encourage problem-solving and physical engagement, strengthening their bond with caregivers.
  2. Socialization: Despite their grumpy facade, Coconut enjoys interactions with zoo staff, showcasing a humorous and food-motivated personality when offered spinach.
  3. Health Monitoring: Our veterinary team provides meticulous care, conducting regular health check-ups to ensure Coconut's well-being and promptly addressing any medical needs.
  4. Educational Outreach: Coconut participates in educational programs, charming visitors with their unique personality and raising awareness about giraffe conservation.
